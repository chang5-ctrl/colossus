# Future Expansion Strategy

> The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.
> We are planting trees that will bear fruit for decades.

## Philosophy

1. **Extensibility by design:** Every component can be replaced or extended
2. **Standards-driven:** Build on open standards, contribute to new ones
3. **Community-powered:** The ecosystem grows faster than any single team
4. **Technology-agnostic:** Don't bet on one technology, bet on interfaces
5. **Long-term thinking:** Decisions made for 2030, not 2026
